CheerMeApp Music Makes The Mood

Acknowledging your emotional state is a staple of good psychological and physiological well-being.

Emotional avoidance is harmful.

As we are exploring new interaction methods between people and computers, we can explore the interaction of mind to brain... or 'Emotional Acceptance'

According to recent psychological research one of the main causes of many psychological problems is the habit of emotional avoidance. This may seem surprising, because the attempt to avoid negative emotions appears to be a reasonable thing.

After all, negative emotions don't feel good, and they are often linked in our minds to negative events that we want to avoid or forget. Moreover, we are all familiar with the momentary relief that avoidance can provide.

It is important to recognize your emotional state.

CheerMeApp does just that.

When it comes to music, your brain imposes structure and order on a sequence of sounds that, in effect, creates an entirely new system of meaning. The appreciation of music is tied to the ability to process its underlying structure- the ability to predict what will occur next in the song. It also allows us to not only analyze emotion, but direct it, and as time comes past- better control it.
